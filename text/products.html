TinX
TinX stands for "TinX is not XSLT". This is a B2B product for developers that allows them to use an alternative to XSLT to peroform XML transformations. The design is based on handling requests for XML document processing such as OFX, IFX, ACORD, and UBL standards. To learn more about TinX, click here for more information or email us at info@lse.com.
